About our restuarent

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day. Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.
